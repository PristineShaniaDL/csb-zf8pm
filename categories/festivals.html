Minasa Festival

The Minasa Festival is the official festival of Bustos, Bulacan Province. It is held in the second week of January, aligned with the Sto. Niño.

Kneeling Carabao Festival

Kneeling Carabao Festival is a unique festival held in the municipality of Pulilan, Bulacan every 14th of May.

Luyang Dilaw Festival

Luyang Dilaw or Halamang Dilaw Festival celebrates the town's prosperity by paying tribute to nature's wonder root crop called “luyang dilaw” (turmeric ginger).
