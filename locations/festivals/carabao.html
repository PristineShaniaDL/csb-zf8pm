Kneeling Carabao Festival

Kneeling Carabao Festival is a unique festival held in the municipality of Pulilan, Bulacan every 14th of May. Even nearby provinces participate in this two-day celebration to join with the parade of beautifully decorated carts.

What to Know?

Activities

There will be a colorful parade of carabao's and streed dance. You will surely entartain to the artistic paintings in the carabao.
 
 
 

Delicacies

A vegan/vegetarian version of Chicharon, the Mushroom Chicharon. You get the crispiness and great taste without having to worry about high cholesterol.

Hotels

Aerostop Hotel
is the nearest hotel/rest house from Pulilan Bulacan. They also provide a lot of services for afordable pricing that match in your budget.
 

Transportation

If you're from Manila then you'll need to use Busses to go to Pulilan, Bulacan. then use Jeepney to travel the whole town.
 
 

Expenses

You'll need to have at least P2,000 - P3,000 pocket money for a trip to Bustos, Bulacan. This will include Foods and more.
