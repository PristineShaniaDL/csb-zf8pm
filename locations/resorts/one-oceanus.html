What to Know?

Activities

The main activities in this resorts are mainly Swimming and sight-seeing. You can relax and enjoy the amazing view in this resort.
 
 
 

Delicacies

They offer a lot of choices in foods that you can choose. From american cuisine to chinese, indian and japanese cuisine.
 

Hotels

This is a resort so they have their own room that you can rest and stay-in as your day goes on
 

Transportation

If you're from Manila then you'll need to use Busses to go to Meycauayan, Bulacan. then use Tricicle to be able to go to One Oceanus Resorts.
 
 

Expenses

You'll need to have at least P20,000 - P30,000 pocket money for a one day and night trip in One Oceanus Resort. This will include Foods and more.
 
 

Animals

As of now they not allowed and kind of animals inside of the Resort so this resort are animal free so no worries if you are not that close to the animals.
