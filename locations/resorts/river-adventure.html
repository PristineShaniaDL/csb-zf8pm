River Adventure

Experience the luxuries Mother Nature has to offer over at San Rafael River Adventure in San Rafael, Bulacan. As the name suggests, one of the best attractions in this private resort is thire river cruise where you can enjoy a delicious and authentic Filipino feast on a floating restaurant. They also offer luxurious accommodations like their romantic Stilt Glass Cabin and Glamping Tents that keeps you close to nature all throughout your stay. Without a doubt, this hidden gem is surely one of the best private resorts in Bulacan.
