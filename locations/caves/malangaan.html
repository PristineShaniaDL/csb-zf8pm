Malangaan Cave

Malangaan Cave, a hidden gem located in Tukod, San Rafael, Bulacan, is a perfect place for people looking for unspoiled places near Manila. The cave is not yet commercialized and is also known for its spring water that you get to see before you get to the cave. According to the locals, the cave used to be the hiding place of guerrillas during World War II.ion for bikers as well as local tourists who are into rest.

What to Know?

Activities

There is so many activities that you can enjoy in this area. From mountain climbing, trekking, hikking, and specialy Cave Exploration.
 
 
 

Delicacies

One of the famouse delicacies not just in DRT but in Bulacan is the Chicharon. Its flavor and crunchiness will satisfy your cravings.
 

Hotels

is the nearest to the Malangaan Cave it will save you time. The hotel have many services that will satisfy your staying.
 

Transportation

If you're from Manila then you'll need to use Busses to go to San Rafael, Bulacan. then use Tricicle to be able to go to the Cave.
 
 

Expences

You'll need to have at least P3,000 - P4,000 pocket money for a trip in malangaan Cave. This will include hotels, Foods and more.
 
 

Animals

Animals sight-seeing is not a problem in this area. You'll definitely enjoy to see a wild forest animals.
