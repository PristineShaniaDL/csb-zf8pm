Puning Cave

Puning Cave is one of the most visited caves in Central Luzon. It is situated along the hilly barangay of Bayabas in Do√±a Remedios Trinidad, Bulacan. The location makes it a dream destination for bikers as well as local tourists who are into rest.

What to Know?

Activities

There is so many activities that you can enjoy in this area. From mountain climbing, trekking, hikking, and specialy Cave Exploration.
 
 
 

Delicacies

One of the famouse delicacies not just in DRT but in Bulacan is the Chicharon. Its flavor and crunchiness will satisfy your cravings.
 

Hotels

Green Garden Court
is the nearest hotel/rest house from Mt. Lumot, They also provide a lot of services for afordable pricing that match in your budget.
 

Transportation

If you're from Manila then you'll need to use Busses to go to DRT, Bulacan. then use Tricicle to be able to go to the Cave.
 
 

Expences

You'll need to have at least P3,000 - P4,000 pocket money for a trip in Puning Cave. This will include hotels, Foods and more.
 
 

Animals

Animals sight-seeing is not a problem in this area. You'll definitely enjoy to see a wild forest animals.
