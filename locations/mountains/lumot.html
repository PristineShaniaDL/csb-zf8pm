What to Know?

Activities

There are so many activities that you'll enjoy like rock climbing, camping, mountain biking, mountain climbing, bushwalking, bird watching and so on.
 
 
 

Delicacies

DRT or Dona Remedios Trinidad is know for their strong yet aromatic Coffee beans that famous in many part on the province and localy known as "Kapeng Tagalog".
 

Hotels

Green Garden Court
is the nearest hotel/rest house from Mt. Lumot, They also provide a lot of services for afordable pricing that match in your budget.
 

Transportation

If you're from Manila then you'll need to use Busses to go to DRT Bulacan then use Tricicle to be able to go to Mt. Lumot.
 
 

Expenses

You'll need to have at least P5,000 - P6,000 pocket money for a one day and night trip in Mt. Lumot. This will include hotels and more.
 
 

Animals

This mountain are popular to Bird Watching. There is so many species of Bird in this area special the Scale-feathered Malkoha.
