Mt. Manalmon

Mt. Manalmon located at sitio Madlum in Brgy. Sibul, San Miguel, Bulacan is highly recommended, especially for beginners. It belongs to trail class 1-2 with 2/9 level of difficulty, minor climb only. Mt. Manalmon day hike is one of the activities you can enjoy in the historical Biak-na-Bato national park for almost 1-hour hike passing through natural trails, cave, river and wonderful rock formations.

What to Know?

Activities

There are so many activities that you'll enjoy like Trekking, Hicking, Swiming and Zipline
 
 
 

Delicacies

San Miguel Bulacan are know for sweet and sticky desert known as "puto", also they are know for their crunchy "Chicharon" but the most famous one is their "Pastilias".
 

Hotels

San Mig Rest House
is the nearest hotel/rest house from Mt. Manalmon, They also provide a lot of services for afordable pricing that match in your budget.
 

Transportation

If you're from Manila then you'll need to use Busses to go to Bulacan, San Miguel then use Tricicle to be able to go to Mt. Manalmon.
 
 

Expences

You'll need to have at least P4,000 - P6,000 pocket money for a one day and night trip in Mt. Manalmon. This will include hotels and more.
 
 

Animals

There are so many Species of Animals that you can see in this area specialy an OWL.
