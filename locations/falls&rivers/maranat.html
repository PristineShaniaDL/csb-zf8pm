Mt. Maranat

Challenge yourself to do a trek with a rewarding view at the end! Unbeknownst to many, Mt. Maranat is actually the name of the falls at the foot of Mt. Oriod. Instead of reaching the summit, youâ€™re actually descending to reach the campsite where the falls await you. During your descent, you will pass through stunning scenes of green forests, rolling terrains, streams, and pools. While all that will definitely elevate the experience, the highlight of your trek will surely be the majestic falls framed by fruit-bearing trees and fallen logs waiting at the end of the path.

What to Know?

Activities

There is so many activities that you can enjoy in this area. From mountain climbing, trekking, ziplinning, swimming, hikking, sight-seeing and many more.
 
 
 

Delicacies

Norzagaray is blessed with natural beauty and is also known for their native kakanins or rice delicacies. It's sweet and sticky rice delicacies called "Casava Cake".
 

Hotels

The nearest hotels that you can find in the area is the "Hotel Turista" at San Jose Del Monte Bulacan.
 

Transportation

If you're from Manila then you'll need to use Busses to go to Norzagaray, Bulacan then use Tricicle to be able to go to Mt. Maranat.
 
 

Expences

You'll need to have at least P5,000 - P8,000 pocket money for a trip in Mt. Maranat. This will include hotels, Foods and more.
 
 

Animals

Animals sight-seeing is not a problem in this area. You'll definitely enjoy to see a wild forest animals.
