Casa Real

Casa Real is a shrine dedicated to the 20 women of Malolos who lobbied to establish the first adult school for women in 1889, despite much resistance from the Spanish friars and even the governor-general of the Philippines. For their efforts, they were praised by reformists Jose Rizal, Graciano Lopez Jaena and Marcelo H. del Pilar.
